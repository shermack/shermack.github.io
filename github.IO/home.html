From Python Novice to Cybersecurity Aficionado

Beginning my programming journey independently with Python, I quickly found myself drawn to back-end web development. As I explored databases and servers, my fascination grew, leading me to a profound interest in cybersecurity. Writing scrapers and manipulating databases not only honed my skills but also fueled my passion for the field. Diving deep into cybersecurity, I adopted Kali Linux as my key operating system, leveraging its robust tools and developing advanced ones in Python. My journey is a testament to self-driven learning and the exciting intersection of web development and cybersecurity.
